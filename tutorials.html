key binds

players will use multiple buttons for each direction to enable double or triple turns. heres some examples of beginner friendly keybind setups to try.

keeping left turns on your left hand and right turns on your right hand is easy to think about and works well for modes like df and ctf.

others prefer a more classic lr lr approach, it comes down to personal preference mostly.
